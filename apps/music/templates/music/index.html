Bio

Bre is as much of a verteran of the music business as one can be at 21 years old. Having performed under many different stage names, including Breanna Lynn, and with her band Bre-L, this newest iteration is taking place after two years of radio silence. But now she's back with all new material, bringing some of her most intimate material and heartfelt lyrics to share. Her newest EP, 'Getting Real' is a compliation of songs about what it's like going through your twenties, understanding the complexities of what it's like growing up in a pressure filled society, and the excitement of the future ahead. Follow Bre on social media to keep up to date about her newest material, upcoming shows, and exciting news!
